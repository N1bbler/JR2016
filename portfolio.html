ClusterStor Service Console (Seagate)

The ClusterStor Service Console is used by professional service persons for the guided replacement of Field Replaceable Units (hardware parts) as an important part of a comprehensive RAS (Reliability, Accessibility, and Serviceability) package for customers.

User Interface Designmore_vert
UI Design and Implementationclose

I worked with a small team of talented Architects, Engineers and Product Owner to design, implement and integrate this responsive product UI into the ClusterStor Service Console product. I created the logo, header image, overall layout, graphics, as well as information architecture and design of each individual view. We built it with custom HTML5 and CSS3 that I wrote along with Dojo JS and our custom backend framework.

Behind the scenes UX work included taking input directly from customers, creating user profiles, incorporating Accessibility features and progressive enhancements. Interaction Design work included sketches, wireframes, and functional prototypes.

The Service Console interface received rave reviews from both internal and external customers.

Sonexion Service Console (Cray)

The Sonexion Service Console is used by professional service persons for the guided replacement of Field Replaceable Units (hardware parts) as an important part of a comprehensive RAS (Reliability, Accessibility, and Serviceability) package for customers.

Product Brandingmore_vert
Product Branding and Implementationclose

Sonexion Service Console is Cray's re-branded product based on ClusterStor Service Console. With input from Cray on the related Sonexion System Manager product, I designed and implemented the product logo, theme colors and overall look and feel. Cray had previously supplied their official logo to be incorporated, which I did to produce the final design.
